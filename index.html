Jiawei Zheng

Postdoctoral Research Fellow

DigitLab

Jiawei Zheng is a Postdoctoral Research Fellow in DigitLab. Prior to this, he obtained PhD degree in AI from University of Edinburgh, supervised by Prof. Jacques Fleuriot, Prof. Jane Hillston and Dr. Petros Papapanagiotou. His PhD is funded by School of Informatics PhD Scholarship. Prior to that, he obtained MEng degree (National Scholarship) in Computer Science from Xidian University, supervised by Prof. Yulong Shen.

His research interests focuses on AI, covering Machine and Deep learning, LLMs, the integration of IoT sensors, blockchain, as well as human machine interactions. His work is dedicated to addressing real-world challenges through applied research in innovative AI methodologies, including process-driven, event-driven, data-driven, and agent-driven systems.

He is interested in make impact across diverse domains, such as manufacturing, healthcare, social care and Activities of Daily Living (ADLs).
