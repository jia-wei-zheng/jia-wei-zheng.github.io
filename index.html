About Me

I am a PhD student at AIML lab in the School of Informatics, University of Edinburgh, supervised by Prof. Jacques Fleuriot, Prof. Jane Hillston and Dr. Petros Papapanagiotou. My PhD is funded by School of Informatics PhD Scholarship. I am a researcher in Artificial Intelligence and its Applications Institute (AIAI) and Advanced Care Research Centre. Prior to that, I obtained MEng degree (National Scholarship) in Computer Science from Xidian University, supervised by Prof. Yulong Shen.

I am conducting research on leveraging domain-specific knowledge (processes) to enhance the performance and interpretability of machine learning models and handling uncertain and probabilistic data.

My research interests focus on integrating pervasive sensors (wearable and ambient sensors) and Neuro-symbolic AI techniques for healthcare and social care problems, applied research of novel methods, such as process-, event-, data-driven systems.
