About Me

I am a PhD student at AIML lab in the School of Informatics, University of Edinburgh. I am a member of Artificial Intelligence and its Applications Institute (AIAI). I am conducting research on machine learning, process mining on the context of manufacturing and Activities of Daily Livings (ADLs). My research interests include Machine Learning, access control in IoT, Blockchain, Business Process Management, Process mining under uncertainty.

I try to avoid low-hanging fruit and tune our baseline models carefully. The function of papers is to generate insight, not fractional improvements in accuracy, and a successful project is one that shapes the way researchers think long after the specific model or task being described goes out of use.
