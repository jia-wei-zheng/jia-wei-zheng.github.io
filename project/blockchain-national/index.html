Intelligent service transaction operation mechanism and performance optimization technology

Apr 27, 272712
Blockchain

This project aims to develope a intelligent service trasaction system based on crowd sourcing and blockchain technologiee so that it can meet the requirements of high performance and supervision.

In particular, we develope a service-oriented and high-precision service publishing and composition system. This enables end-users (e.g., individuals and companies) to publish their services and data in the blockchain as digital asset. This could also provide high-precision service recommendation and service composition regarding to the custimized functions.

We also investigate the approaches to improve the performance of blockchain by optimizing the consensus algorithms and sharding techniques.

A blockchain-based service trading mechanism and system is developed to enable decentralized services transactions on blockchain.
